FAVORITE BAND

Music eases one's mind and it soothes one heart. Music plays a big role in our lives. Though some may listen to music and get entertained,some will get a message from it. Yes, music conveys messages. H_art the band is my favorite band because it has got a different type of taste unlike the rest. Their music is inspiring.The stage performance and their dressing code is always outstanding. The hair style which is locs are at per with the kind of tune they make which is reggae hence blends with what they deliver.

H_ART THE BAND

Just as the name (ART) suggests, the three men try so hard to make their audience happy with what they deliver. The band was formed in 2012 when they met at KNT during a play on Cancer Awareness. It was during the auditions that Mordecai sang and both Kenneth and Wachira knew that they could sing. They later came together and formed their band which was later signed by the Penya Africa. The three are;

  1. Wachira
  2. Kenneth
  3. Mordecai

My favorite songs sang by them include;

  • Uliza Kiatu
  • Nikikutazama
  • El Shaddai
  • Masheesha

Other songs that have been sung by the three are;

  • Issa Vibe
  • Rosella
  • Lover Lover
  • Linda Moyo

WACHIRA GATAMA

Wachira,popularly known as (Chirah) hails from Kayole in Nairobi,Kenya. In the band, he mainly does the spoken word which he brings it into pop. He had broken up with his girlfriend of many years before the album project began. He is the most emotional person in the band and this drives him in doing poetry.

KENNETH MUYA MUKHWANA

Kenneth is popularly known as Kenchez. He comes from Kayole, Nairobi. He is the guitarist of the band. Also, he compliments the rhymes with smooth and danceable.

MORDECAI KIMEU

He is popularly known as Mordecai. He comes from Kayole, Nairobi. He is best known as a poet.In the band, he compliments the jam with his vocals and lyrics. He has married and got a pretty daughter. Mordecai revealed that he could walk from Kayole to Pangani just for the auditions. He later became thier lead singer in the band.
